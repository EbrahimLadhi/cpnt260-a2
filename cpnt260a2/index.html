Rome

The Colosseum is oval shaped ampithitheatre. It is 189 meters long, 156 meters wide, and 48,5 meters tall. The entire building has a surface area of 6 acres.

Grand Canyon

The youngest layer of the canyon—the Kaibab—is 270 million years old, while the oldest layers date back as far as 1.8 billion years.

Maldives

The Maldives is a chain of 26 coral atolls. There are 1,200 small islands in the Maldives, of these only about 200 are inhabited.
